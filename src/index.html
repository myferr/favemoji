Emoji Favicon Generator

Try visiting /🧑‍💻 or /📚 to see the emoji as a favicon.

Here are some more examples:

/🛂 /🕶️ /🔔 /💻

Wait.. this also works with letters?!

Yeah! It's crazy 🤪

Up to 4 letters can use used as a favicon yet up to 2-3 letters are displayed

Here are some examples:

/f /aa /abcd /abc

So, how do I use it?

Just add this to your index.html file:

<link rel="icon" href="https://favemoji.vercel.app/🧑‍💻" />

or

<link rel="icon" href="https://favemoji.vercel.app/ff" />
